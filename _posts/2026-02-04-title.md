I have a special prayer (karakia) in both Maori and English.

Te Reo Version: 

E te Atua, e tiaki i tō mātou whānau,  
Whakaritea mātou ki te noho haumaru i ngā rā katoa.  
Awhinatia mātou ki te whakawhirinaki ki a tētahi ki tētahi,  
Ārahina mātou kia noho ngātahi i te aroha me te kotahitanga.  
Kia tau te rangimārie ki roto i ō mātou whare,  
Āmene.

English Version:

God, watch over our family,  
Keep us safe every day.  
Help us to trust one another,  
Guide us to live together in love and unity.  
May peace rest within our homes,  
Amen.
